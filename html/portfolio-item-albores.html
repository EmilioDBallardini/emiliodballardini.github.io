Albores

Explore the history of computation in this ubiquitous experience

The idea was proposed by the LIDI at the Informatics College with an educational porpose.

The main concept of this project is to bring information about the origins and evolution of computational science closer to younger students, focusing mainly in elementary & intermediate school.
Going from the very beginnings of logical machinery capable of solving simple and very specific problems, to analogical machines used to solve algorithmics ecuations, integrals and derivatives.
And finishing of with with the invention and scaling of the modern computer, that gave the posibility of you seeing this!
